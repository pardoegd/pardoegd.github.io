Sunrise Beach

This Scene Design was made using Unity and 3D Studio Max, another Scene Design, however this one was to celebrate the start of Summer and to celebrate the amaxing weather that the UK had been getting, this was a simple tropical beach scene that was inspired by the beaches in Crysis and Just Cause as the both of these games had amazing beach scenery. Textures were from textures.com and arroway textures, Tumblr and Pinterest, other assets were from the Unity Asset Store.
