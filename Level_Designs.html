Level Designs

Welcome to my Level Designs Page, below and on the other pages linked below you will be able to see all of my Level and Scene Designs that I have done using various Game Engines over the past couple of years. Some of the 3D Models that you see were create by me using a mixture of both 3D Studio Max and Blender. The other assets that were used in these Scene/Level Designs were from the Unity Asset Store. The textures that I have used to build up the materials of the models were from a mixture Textures.com, arroway-textures and Dev-Assets. So, yeah, feel free to gander through the screenshots below and follow me on social media as that is the best way to keep up to date with all of my latest content. There are also 4K Downloads available for each of the screenshots shown below, these can be downloaded through my Flickr page, which is linked in the footer of each page in this site.



Unity Level Designs

The below section is filled with all of my Level Designs that I have done using the Unity Engine, I have used multiple versions of the Unity Engine below, from Unity 2018 to Unity 2020.



Night Train

Decided to do another night scene, as I love to experiment with the lighting in these types of scenes, to see how close to reality I can get it, this time I did a train station at night on the outskirts of the city. One thing that I loved experimenting around with was the light hue over the city skyline as I had to mess about with multiple settings for the light to get it the way I wanted it, but also the overall idea of having multiple lighting sources in the scene and how even just changing one light can affect the overall environment. As usual feel free to comment down below what you liked and what you think could be added or improved, as I would love to increase my knowledge in the lighting areas of Unity. The assets and textures that were not made by me came from the Unity Asset Store and Textures.com.
