Abandoned Stargate

This Scene Design was made using Unity and 3D Studio Max, this was my second level design. I decided to do this Scene/Level to celebrate the 20th Anniversary of Stargate SG1. Textures were from textures.com and arroway textures, Tumblr and Pinterest, other assets were from the Unity Asset Store.
