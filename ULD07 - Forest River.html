Forest River

This Scene Design was made using Unity and 3D Studio Max, at the time of releasing this scene design we were just entering Spring, so like with the past seasons and most likely all future seasons, I created a scene/level to reflect the current season, this was also made as I took a break from a big project that I was working on in the background that will be revealed soon!! Textures were from textures.com and arroway textures, Tumblr and Pinterest, other assets were from the Unity Asset Store.
