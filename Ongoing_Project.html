CURRENT PROJECT: Unamed Level/Game ;D

This is a big project that I am currently working on, this is currently in the prototype phase, so I am creating a prototype level for the overall idea, once this one scene is done I will then start on the overall level that will be featured in the game. Below are the separate phases that the final and even the prototype went through to get to the final product that you see at the end. The overall plan for this level is for it to turn into a fully fledge game, where you will be able to go between the multiple maps on the fly, through various mechanics and Easter Eggs. However, I have not yet decided what I would like to do with the final levels and overall game, but it will defiantly be some form of FPS Game.

Prototype Level (Phase 01)

Below are the final test renders of the first phase of the prototype, as you can see all of the models have been made but no materials have yet been put onto them. All of the models and the room itself is to scale so they have been made using the real-world measurements and were created and rendered using Blender3D. This currently the first phase of the prototype the next phase will be with all of the materials mapped correctly onto the models, also after this room is done the rest of the floor of this building will be made up before it is imported into the Unity Engine.
