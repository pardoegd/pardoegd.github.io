CURRENT PROJECT: Abandoned ;D

Abandoned Phase 01

This is my latest project that I am working on, currently I have only partially done the main structure of the building, but the overall plan is for this to be an abandoned building in the middle of an apocalyptic world. I will not only create the overall level and allow the player to be able to walk around the level, but I hopefully will also be able to create some form of story and missions to increase the narrative. I plan of sharing every stage of the development here and even on all of the social media platforms linked in the footer, if you would like to comment on how the level looks so far, or even have any suggestions for the level, as this will help me be able to create a final amazing level that will hopefully be available to download and play through.
This has been inspired by a multitude of real-world buildings, abandoned and not, but also games like Crysis 2 and The Last of Us, the assets that were not made by me came from the Unity Asset Store and the textures came from Textures.com.

Abandoned Secret Underground Bunker Easter Egg

Another update of the project that I am working on, this time some experimentation and even a glimpse of a small Easter egg that might be included within the final level. I was experimenting with a volumetric add on for Unity (Aura), which works amazingly well in bringing the scene to life, a more polished version of this might be available somewhere in the final game as a small Easter egg, along with some more areas and even more Easter eggs included.
