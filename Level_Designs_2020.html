Level Designs

Welcome to my Level Designs Page for the year of 2020, below and on the other pages linked below you will be able to see all of my Level and Scene Designs that I have done using various Game Engines over the past year. Some of the 3D Models that you see were create by me using a mixture of both 3D Studio Max and Blender. The other assets that were used in these Scene/Level Designs were from the Unity Asset Store. The textures that I have used to build up the materials of the models were from a mixture Textures.com, arroway-textures and Dev-Assets. So, yeah, feel free to gander through the screenshots below and follow me on social media as that is the best way to keep up to date with all of my latest content. There are also 4K Downloads available for each of the screenshots shown below, these can be downloaded through my Flickr page, which is linked in the footer of each page in this site.



Unity Level Designs

The below section is filled with all of my Level Designs that I have done using the Unity Engine, I have used multiple versions of the Unity Engine below, from Unity 2018 to Unity 2019.



Forest Winter Wonderland

To celebrate the winter months, I decided to create this winter scene within Unity, this time just a simple cabin in the woods filled with snow scene, a typical shot that is taken at winter and Christmas. Personally, I love scenes and photos like these, especially when you have the morning sun just poking through the trees with just a tad of morning mist. Would love to go somewhere like this one day, hopefully when this lockdown stuff is over.

12th Doctor TARDIS Interior

Decided to take another break from the main project and challenge myself and see if I could recreate the 12th doctors TARDIS Console Room, overall, I am extremely happy with how the final project looks, especially with the lighting. This is one of my favorite TARDIS Interiors from the show alongside tens and thirteens, due to the overall design and detail included within the room. Look out for future posts as I might come back to this project in the future to add interactions, animations and even the outer shell. As usual feel free to comment what you like the best and what could be improved on within the scene, the assets and textures that were not made by me came from the Unity Asset Store, Textures.com and Pinterest.
