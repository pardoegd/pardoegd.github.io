Abandoned TARDIS

This Scene Design was made using Unity and 3D Studio Max, this was my first proper attempt at level design. I decided to do this to celebrate the reveal of the new Doctor. Textures were from textures.com and arroway textures, the two TARDIS Textures were from Tumblr and Pinterest, other assets were from the Unity Asset Store.

Just decided to make the TARDIS, within 3DS Max and Unity, not the best model or scene, but still looks pretty good, for a first attempt with not much reference material. This was to celebrate the new doctor being revealed, cannot wait for the Christmas episode and the next series. (Textures are from Textures.com and arroway textures, plus the two TARDIS ones from Tumblr and Pinterest. Other assets are from the unity asset store.
