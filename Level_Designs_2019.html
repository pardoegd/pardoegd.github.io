Level Designs

Welcome to my Level Designs Page for the year of 2018, below and on the other pages linked below you will be able to see all of my Level and Scene Designs that I have done using various Game Engines over the past year. Some of the 3D Models that you see were create by me using a mixture of both 3D Studio Max and Blender. The other assets that were used in these Scene/Level Designs were from the Unity Asset Store. Winter in the Park

Decided to take another break from the main project to work on something festive, this time I decided to create a scene based on one of the most iconic and one of my personal favorite winter scenes, Winter in Central Park, New York. This allowed me to experiment with certain Unity assets even further and even mess around with snow once again. Overall, I decided to only have a small scene as there are many beautiful areas of Central Park, the Small Pond and Bridge, as well as a Lake-Side walkway alongside the iconic City Skyscraper background. The assets that I did not make came from the Unity Asset Store and the Texture came from Textures.com.

Cottage Winter Wonderland

Another Level/Scene Design, been wanting to create a scene with snow in for a very long time, finally go chance to do it recently and here it is. The inspiration mostly came from the English countryside, and some other photos that I had seen of random countryside’s. this scene did not only allow me to be able to create a scene with some snow, but also, I was able to learn a new way to create buildings that have no interior. The assets that were not made by me came from the Unity Asset Store and the textures came from Textures.com
